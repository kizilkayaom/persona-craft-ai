# Ethical Considerations

## Overview

PersonaCraft AI generates marketing content using Large Language Models (LLMs). While these models provide fast and creative outputs, responsible AI principles must be considered throughout the generation process.

---

# Potential Risks

## Hallucination

The language model may generate product specifications or claims that are not provided by the user.

Mitigation

- Prompt explicitly instructs the model not to invent specifications.
- Marketing content focuses only on the given product information.

---

## Misleading Advertising

AI-generated text may unintentionally exaggerate product capabilities.

Mitigation

- The system prompt prevents misleading claims.
- Users are responsible for reviewing generated content before publication.

---

## Audience Bias

Different personas may unintentionally reinforce stereotypes.

Mitigation

- Persona definitions focus on communication style instead of demographic assumptions.
- Prompts avoid discriminatory or offensive language.

---

## Privacy

The system does not require personal user information.

Mitigation

- Only product name
- Audience category
- Output type

are processed.

No personal or sensitive data is stored.

---

## Human Oversight

Generated marketing content should always be reviewed by a human before being published.

The application is designed as a decision-support tool rather than a fully autonomous marketing system.

---

# Responsible AI Principles

The project follows these principles:

- Transparency
- Fairness
- Human oversight
- Privacy awareness
- Responsible content generation
- Prompt-based safety instructions

---
